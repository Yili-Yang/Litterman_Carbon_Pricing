   9

  12 
%   FMINCON.
  13

  15

  16

  17 
%   a scalar, vector, or matrix. 
  18 
%
  19 
%   X = FMINCON(FUN,X0,A,B,Aeq,Beq) minimizes FUN subject to the linear 
  20

  21 
%   inequalities exist.)
  22 
%
  23

  24

  25

  30
